Our story

Foresight, diversification and encountering global quality furnitures ensured our process of forward integration from Importer and whole seller of furnitures to a leading retail brand in the city of Madurai, Tamil Nadu.

Arrow Furniture established in the year 1991 by two decades experienced, furniture professional Mr. M. Karunakaran to cater the furniture needs of people in and around Madurai. The first showroom spanning an area of 8500 sq. ft of fully air conditioned floor area with abundant parking facilities was started in 1991.
